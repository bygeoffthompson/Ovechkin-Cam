Assist Search

All assist data is stored in format [Name] Primary [Name] Secondary

  • Evgeny Kuznetsov Primary
  • Mike Green Secondary
  • Nicklas Backstrom Primary TJ Oshie Secondary

Date Search

  • [Month DD YYYY] Format
    • January 13 2010
    • March 31 2015
    • November 5 2022

Goal Search

All goals are identified by a unique number. NHL regular season goals are indexed 1 to 919. All other goals are intertwined as floats with format [NHL Regular Goal].[Other Goal]

  • 0.02 is World Championship Goal #2 (2005)
  • 36.04 is Olympic Goal #4 (2006)
  • 288.05 is All Star Goal #5 (2011)
  • 525.02 is World Cup Goal #2 (2016)
  • 897.75 is Playoff Goal #75 (2025)

In a few cases this cataloging produced duplicate floats which were resolved by incrementing the NHL number by one.

League Search

  • "All Star" for All Star Goals
  • "KHL" for KHL Goals
  • "Olympic" for Olympic Goals
  • "Playoff" for NHL Playoff Goals
  • "Regular" for NHL Regular Season Goals
  • "World Championship" for World Championship Goals
