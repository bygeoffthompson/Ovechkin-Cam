About Ovechkin Cam

This app is a catalog of Ovechkin goal videos from first to last (904) including All Star Game (8), Playoff (77), and KHL (19) goals for a total of 1,008.

Our searchable sequential index allows users to find Ovechkin goals in a new and useful way.

Ovechkin.cam accepts a URL query to retrieve specific goals (?1 to ?904) or a set of filtered search results (?fleury+vegas).

Text Search Tips:

  • "All Star" for ASG Goals
  • "Century" for Hundredth Goals
  • "Empty Net" for Empty Net Goals
  • "First" for Season Starting Goals
  • "GWG" for Game Winning Goals
  • "Last" For Season Ending Goals
  • "Overtime" for Overtime Goals
  • "Playoff" for Playoff Goals
  • "PPG" for Power Play Goals
  • "SHG" for Shorthanded Goals
