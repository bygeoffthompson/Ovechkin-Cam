About Ovechkin Cam

Ovechkin Cam is a catalog of goal videos from first to last (911) that also includes All Star Game (8), Playoff (77), IIHF (35), KHL (19), and Olympic (8) goals for a total of 1,058.

Our searchable and sequential index allows users to find Ovi goals in a new and useful way.

Ovechkin.cam accepts a URL query to retrieve specific goals (?1 to ?911) or a set of filtered search results (?fleury+golden-knights). A type filter can be appended to further reduce results (?fleury+golden-knights|playoff).

Date Search

  • [Month DD YYYY] Format
    • January 13 2010
    • March 31 2015
    • November 5 2022

League Search

  • [All Star] for All Star Goals or use query filter |allstar
  • [IIHF] for IIHF/World Championship Goals or use query filter |iihf
  • [KHL] for KHL Goals or use query filter |khl
  • [Olympic] for Olympic Goals or use query filter |olympic
  • [Playoff] for Playoff Goals or use query filter |playoff
  • [World Cup] for World Cup Goals or use query filter |worldcup

Number Search

All goals are identified by a unique integer. NHL regular season goals are indexed 1 to 911.

All other goals are intertwined as floats with the format [NHL-Goals].[League-Goals]

  • 36.01 Olympic Goal #1 During NHL Season 1
  • 288.05 All Star Goal #5 During NHL Season 6
  • 525.02 World Cup Goal #2 Before NHL Season 12
  • 897.75 Playoff Goal #75 After NHL Season 20

In a few cases this indexing produced duplicate floats. In this event the latter was made negative to remain distinct (-339.26).
