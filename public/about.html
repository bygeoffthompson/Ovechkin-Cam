About Ovechkin Cam

Ovechkin Cam is a catalog of goal videos from first to last that includes All Star (8), Playoff (77), World Championship (35), World Cup (2), KHL (19), and Olympic (8) goals for a total of 1,068.

Our searchable and sequential index allows users to find Ovi goals in a new and useful way.

Date Search

  • [Month DD YYYY] Format
    • January 13 2010
    • March 31 2015
    • November 5 2022

Number Search

All goals are identified by a unique integer. NHL regular season goals are indexed 1 to 919.

All other goals are intertwined as floats with the format [NHL-RS].[Other]

  • 0.02 is World Championship Goal #2 (2005)
  • 36.04 is Olympic Goal #4 (2006)
  • 288.05 is All Star Goal #5 (2011)
  • 525.02 is World Cup Goal #2 (2016)
  • 897.75 is Playoff Goal #75 (2025)

In a few cases this cataloging produced duplicate floats which were resolved by incrementing the NHL number by one.

League Search

  • "All Star" for All Star Goals
  • "IIHF" for World Championship Goals
  • "KHL" for KHL Goals
  • "Olympic" for Olympic Goals
  • "Playoff" for NHL Playoff Goals
  • "Regular" for NHL Regular Season Goals
  • "World Cup" for World Cup Goals
