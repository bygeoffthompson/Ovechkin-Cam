About Ovechkin Cam

Ovechkin Cam is a catalog of goal videos from first to last (911) that includes All Star Game (8), Playoff (77), and KHL (19) goals for a total of 1,015.

Ovechkin.cam accepts a URL query to retrieve specific goals (?1 to ?911) or a set of filtered search results (?fleury+vegas).

Our searchable sequential index allows users to find Ovi goals in a new and useful way.

Goal Search

  • "30th" for Thirtieth Goals

  • "Last" For Season Ending Goals
  • "All Star" for ASG Goals
  • "Rocket" for Rocket Richard Winning Goals
  • "Century" for Hundredth Goals
  • "Empty Net" for Empty Net Goals
  • "GWG" for Game Winning Goals
  • "PPG" for Power Play Goals
  • "SHG" for Shorthanded Goals
  • "Overtime" for Overtime Goals
  • "Multi" for Second Goals
  • "Hat Trick" for Third Goals
  • "4th" for Fourth Goals
  • "Playoff" for Playoff Goals
  • "Teammate" for Former Teammate Goals
