About Ovechkin.cam

This app is a catalog of Ovechkin goals from first to last (899) that includes All Star Game (8) and Playoff (77) goals.

Our sequential index allows users to search through Ovechkin goals in a new and useful way.

Ovechkin.cam accepts queries that link directly to specific goals (?1 to ?899) or a set of filtered results (?fleury+vegas).

Search Tips:

  • "All Star" for ASG Goals
  • "Empty Net" for Empty Net Goals
  • "First" for Season Starting Goals
  • "GWG" for Game Winning Goals
  • "Last" For Season Ending Goals
  • "Overtime" for Overtime Goals
  • "Playoff" for Playoff Goals
  • "PPG" for Power Play Goals
  • "SHG" for Shorthanded Goals
